Weight in kg * 10 + height in cm * 6.25 - 5 * age + 5

BMR * 1.2 (Here 1.2 is for 1-3 hours of activity per week)

**Preworkout**

- Eat 30-40g of protein within last 3 hours pre-workout
- Eat 30-40g of carbs 15-60 minutes pre-workout
    - Eg. Fruit, starch, simple sugar
    - Author's favorite
        - Oatmeal, bananas, dates, figs, melons, white potatoes, white rice, raisins and sweet potatoes

**Post workout**

- Eat 30-40g of protein within 1-2 hours
- Maybe eat carbs after workout. No big deal
- Maybe some fat. Doesn't do any harm

**Ultimate workout plan**

Formula

2-3: Train 2-3 major muscle groups per week

4-6: Do 4-6 reps per hard set

9-15: Do 9-15 hard sets per workout

2-4: Rest 2-4 minutes between sets

3-5: Train most major muscle groups once every 3-5 days

1-2: Take 1-2 days off the week

8-10: Take it easy every 8-10 weeks

Double progression model

Rep-tempo 1-1-1 for all weight lifting exercises

Warm up is more of a practice than just tuning to the right body temperature. Do several warm up sets with the first exercise for each of those muscle groups.

10. Do 10 reps with about 50% of your hard set weight and rest for a minute.
11. Do 10 reps with the same weight at a slightly faster pace and rest for a minute.
12. Do 4 reps with about 70% weight of your hard set weight and rest for a minute.

The deload week - Super compensation

- A week long reduction in training intensity to recover better
- A recommendation is to deload every 8-10 weeks

If something hurts, stop immediately. To know if it is really pain or just a discomfort,

- Check if it hits on both sides of the body. If it is one side, then it is pain.
- Is the pain concentrated around a joint or specific spot?
    - If the pain lingers, back off and rest the affected areas.

**To break through muscle gain plateaus**

Increase calorie intake by 150 calories by increasing carbohydrate

Limit your intake of low calorie food

Increase calorie dense food like bread, pancakes, pasta, rice, oatmeal, granola, cereal and muesli. Also add sauces and condiments to meals when possible. Add 2 tablespoon of honey or maple syrup.

Keep total free sugar intake below 50 grams per day.

Sugar free calorie drink includes - milk, unsweetened rice milk, unsweetened almond milk or coconut water.
