Vacation is earned at the end of each pay period.

Paid personal time 

- 48 hours awarded each year
- 10 initial; 8 for each month until 48
- 48 hours can be carried over
- Any excess hours are carries over to sick time
- maximum 96 hours
- So make sure each year it is ≤ 48

Vacation time 

- 160 hours awarded each year for 6+ years of tenure
- 13.34 hours per month for 12 months
- Limited to 160 hours each year
- 160 hours can be carried over

Sick and safe time

- 24 hours awarded
- No cap
- Carry-over limit is 24 hours
- Any excess hours are carried over to paid personal time
