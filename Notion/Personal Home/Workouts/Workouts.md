# Barbell squat

## Descent

1. Walk toward barbell (not backward)
2. Feet shoulder width
3. Feet at 11 and 1 o clock
4. Narrow grip so that shoulder blades are pinched
5. Take deep breath at the bottom of stomach
6. Brace abs as if you were about to get punched
7. Stare at floor six feet away
8. Push hips back and bend knees (not farther than toes)
9. Controlled descent (not faster)
10. Thighs parallel to ground or lower
11. Back straight or at an angle that puts bar over the middle of feet

## Ascent

12. Drive through heels and middle of your feet
13. Helps to imagine you're gripping the floor with toes and feet like eagle clenching it's claws
14. Squeeze glutes and push hip forward while simultaneously going up
15. Begin exhaling

# Barbell deadlift

### Setup

16. Position feet slightly narrower than shoulder width apart with toes pointed slightly out.
17. Stand up tall with chest out and take a deep breath of air into your belly, bracing abs (get punched tight)
18. Move down towards the bar by pushing your hips back, just like squat
19. Arch your lower-back slightly to a half-squat position
20. You should feel considerable tightness in your hamstrings and hips which is desirable
21. Hands on the bar with double overhand grip just outside your shins and squeeze as hard as possible.
22. Press upper arms into your sides as if you were trying to crush oranges in your armpits.
23. Arms should be completely straight and locked
24. Head in neutral position

### Ascent

25. Forcefully drive body upward and slightly back onto heels
26. Push through heels, elbows locked and lower-back slightly arched
27. Ensure hips and shoulders rise simultaneously
28. Push hips towards bar and feel hamstrings working hard
29. Head in neutral position in line with your spine

### Descent

30. Push hips back (not bending knees)
31. Let bar slide down thighs
32. Lower-back should remain locked and core tight
33. Drop it to floor

# Barbell bench press

### Setup

34. Eyes under the bar
35. Raise chest and tuck shoulder blades
36. Grip just little wider than shoulder width
37. Slightly arch lower back and plant feet on the ground (leg-drive for extra strength)
38. Butt always on bench
39. Unrack bar to middle of your chest
40. Don't watch the bar. Pick a spot in ceiling.
41. Take deep breath, push knees apart and squeeze the bar

### Descent

42. Elbows 30 - 60 degree relative to torso
43. Imagine pulling the bar down instead of just dropping.
44. Try pulling the bar apart as you descent

### Ascent

45. Picture pushing torso away from bar instead bar away from your body
46. Push above diagonally to natural position
47. Lock elbows at the top
