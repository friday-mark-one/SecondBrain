**Anxiety management**

1. Greet the anxiety - acknowledge that you are anxious and not let the level climb anymore
2. Reframe the situation as a conversation - make things a casual conversation vs a performance
3. Be present - with a tongue twister

**Ground rules**

4. Get yourself out of your way. (eg. I have to get the answers right for the interview)
    1. Game: Point at things saying something that is different from what you are pointing. (eg. look at a chair and say orange) for 15 - 30 seconds. Practice this several times.
5. Think of the interview as an opportunity rather than an adversary (you vs them).
    2. Game: Give an imaginary gift to your friend. Ask him to say what's in the gift. And your respond saying "I bought you this gift because ... "
6. Slow down and listen
    3. Game: Spell an entire sentence saying what fun thing you are going to do today. - so you listen perfectly in the moment before responding

**Responding to questions**

Respond with proper structure - Problem -> Solution -> Benefit
