I recently had inquiries about my interview journey and how I managed to secure Amazon's SDE2 position. To assist you in your preparation, here are some valuable resources:

🔶 For High-Level Design:

1. "System Design Interview" by Alex Xu (Volume 1 & 2)

2. "Designing Data-Intensive Applications" by Martin Klepmann (excellent for advanced design)

3. Engineering blogs from companies such as Uber, Atlassian, Discord, etc.

🔶 For Low-Level Design:

1. Refactoring Guru: [Refactoring Guru Design Patterns](

[https://lnkd.in/gNtwh8u7](https://lnkd.in/gNtwh8u7)

)

2. My personal repository containing basic design pattern questions for practice, with more questions to be added in the upcoming weeks: [Design Patterns Repository](

[https://lnkd.in/g_JUdiY2](https://lnkd.in/g_JUdiY2)

)

3. "Head First Design Patterns" by Eric Freeman, Elisabeth Robson, Bert Bates, Kathy Sierra

Additional tips:

🔸 Consider purchasing LeetCode Premium for access to company-specific questions

🔸 Review interview experiences at the companies you are targeting to understand their expectations

🔸 Conduct mock interviews for practice

🔸 Undertake meaningful projects in your current role and articulate them effectively, especially for design-related discussions
