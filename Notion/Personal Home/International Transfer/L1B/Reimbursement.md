### REIMBURSEMENT POLICY

Amazon will reimburse foreign national employees and their dependents for mandatory U.S. visa application fees

---

associated with obtaining and/or renewal of their U.S. visa stamp at a U.S. Consulate or U.S. Embassy up to $500.

---

These fees do not include incidentals such as lodging, gas, and meals. If the business requires the employee to have a

visa stamp to travel (for business related purposes, internationally) Amazon may, at its discretion, choose to

reimburse the employee for additional expenses associated with obtaining a visa stamp. These expenses may or may

not include travel and accommodations. All business travel must be pre-approved by the employee’s manager as per

the company’s corporate travel policy

- * To submit a reimbursement request, you should use the iExpense tool. An immigration-specific category does not
exist in the expense report. As such, we recommend you follow these instructions:
1. Be sure you have a printout of the visa fee paid online to the Consulate
2. Use the default TRAVEL AND OTHER EXPENSES reimbursement template
3. Enter the date and receipt amount as a line item
4. In the Expense Type field select Miscellaneous
5. In the Justification field type in “visa-related fees”
6. Upload a scanned copy of the receipt as an attachment
7. Submit the expense report to your manager for approval
