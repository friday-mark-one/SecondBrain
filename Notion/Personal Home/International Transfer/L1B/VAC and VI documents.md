### Checklist of documents (from Email)

VISA STAMPING DOCUMENT CHECKLIST

-  1. Original Form I-797, approval notice
-  2. Copy of the petition packet that was the basis for your visa approval, either filed with USCIS in the U.S. or
presented at the border or U.S. Consulate abroad.
-  3. Original, valid passport, which should be valid for at least six months.
    - *REMINDER: Please always renew your passport at the earliest opportunity possible because this may
impact the validity of your I-94 once you are admitted into the U.S.
-  4. Original education documents, if available. Copies will normally suffice and are included in the copy of your
petition.
-  5. Employment Verification Letter (EVL) from Amazon.

 You can create an account at *The Work Number *and print your own EVL approximately two weeks

prior to your travel.

-  6. Updated resume with Amazon experience and job title.
-  7. Your three most recent paystubs, which you can access here.
-  8. One original digital passport style photo (you may also need to upload a digital photo with your DS-160
application prior to submission).
-  9. Visa fee payment receipt. (Please be sure to check the Consulate’s website for information on payment of
the visa fees, as these procedures vary greatly.)
