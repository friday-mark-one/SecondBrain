> [!note]+ Exp19
> I got my visa approved along with NIE travel. First to get approved in the session today at Delhi embassy. So here are the near-miss saves I had
> 
> 113. You need to carry passport, i797, employment verification letter, pay check or yearly remuneration paper you get during appraisal, offer letter, NIE letter on Amazon letterhead signed by manager (I had to explain why I didn’t have a signature).
> 114. The interviewer asked all these docs while asking questions related to employee name, my role name, my duties (what do you do), how long have I worked with Amazon, where in USA do I work and how much do I earn. That took around 10mins.
> 115. After the visa approval, the interviewer asked for NiE letter, why I needed to be in USA and by what date and why? The interviewer read the letter, asked if my role is related to public health sector and what is the vital support for infrastructure. He then asked abt consequence of me not being there. He pulled in three officers and asked them a few things, typed for a while, went inside the office for something and came back.
> 116. He asked how my role is vital support again and typed some more. And then said my visa is approved, returned all the docs and put a sticky note on pssport stating NiE approved.
> 117. He asked me to collect in 4-5 days. And that I have approval to travel single entry into USA within 30 days from today.
