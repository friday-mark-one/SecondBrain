# Restaurants journal

Now a proper database — **one note per restaurant** under `08-Food/Restaurants/`,
browsable and filterable via **[[Restaurants.base]]** (views by cuisine, location, and vegan).

**Look up a place:** open its note (e.g. `[[Din Tai Fung]]`) — every dish you ordered, both
scores, and any ordering tips on one screen.

**Log a new place:** add a note in `08-Food/Restaurants/` with frontmatter
`type: restaurant` + `cuisine`, `location`, `vegan`, then a `| Dish | Mira | Bharath | Notes |`
table. Revisiting? Just append a row to that restaurant's existing note.
