## Ingredients
- [[Idli batter]] | ~9 idlies
- [[Groundnut oil]] | to grease the plates
- [[Sesame oil]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Mix water and salt into the batter (~½ tsp salt for 9 idlies).
2. Grease the idli plates with groundnut (peanut) oil.
3. Pour batter to fill ~90% of each mould.
4. Steam on high for 20 min.
5. Let it sit for 15 min.
6. Garnish with sesame (gingelly) oil.
