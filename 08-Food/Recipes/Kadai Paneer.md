## Ingredients
- [[Paneer]] | cubed
- [[Onion]] | 2-3 total
- [[Tomato]] | 3-5 total
- [[Capsicum]] | ½-1
- [[Garlic]] | 5 cloves
- [[Ginger]] | 1 inch + julienne
- [[Coriander seeds]] | 2 tsp
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 2 tsp
- [[Fennel seeds]] | 2 tsp
- [[Peppercorns]] | 2 tsp
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 3
- [[Groundnut oil]] | 2 spoons, with ghee
- [[Ghee]] | for cooking
- [[Turmeric]] | ½ tsp
- [[Red chilli powder]] | 1 spoon
- [[Garam masala]] | 1 tsp
- [[Fresh Cream]] | to finish
- [[Kasuri methi]] | to finish
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Kadai masala: dry-roast coriander, jeera, fennel, pepper, dry chilli; coarse-grind.
2. Oil+ghee, cumin; add chopped garlic + ginger, sauté.
3. Add finely chopped onion, brown; turmeric, chilli powder + kadai masala, salt.
4. Add chopped tomato; cook mushy, oil separates.
5. Separately sauté onion/capsicum/tomato petals + paneer with garam masala on high.
6. Combine with the paste, add water, cook 2 min.
7. Finish cream, kasuri methi, coriander, julienne ginger.
