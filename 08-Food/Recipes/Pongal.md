## Ingredients
- [[Crystal sona masoori rice]] | 1 part
- [[Moong dal]] | 1 part
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Pepper powder]] | to taste
- [[Peppercorns]] | for tempering in ghee
- [[Cashews]] | for tempering
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tempering
- [[Ghee]] | for tempering
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Ginger]] | grated

## Directions
1. Wash rice + moong dal 1:1, add 1:3 water (e.g. 1+1 cup → 6 cups).
2. Add salt, asafotida, pepper; cook long.
3. Fry cashews, jeera, peppercorns in ghee; add curry leaves and grated ginger.
