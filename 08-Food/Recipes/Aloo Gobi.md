## Ingredients
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for roasting & tempering
- [[Cauliflower]] | 15 florets
- [[Potato]] | 1, cubed
- [[Tomato]] | 1
- [[Cashews]] | 5-7
- [[Onion]] | 1
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tadka
- [[Cinnamon]] | 1 piece
- [[Cloves]] | 2
- [[Ginger garlic paste]] | 1 tbsp
- [[Turmeric]] | ½ tsp
- [[Red chilli powder]] | 1 tsp
- [[Cumin powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Coriander powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Curd]] | 2 tbsp
- [[Garam masala]] | ½ tsp
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish
- [[Kasuri methi]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Roast cauliflower and potato in oil on medium.
2. Grind tomato + cashews to puree.
3. Oil + jeera, cinnamon, cloves; sauté onion.
4. Add ginger garlic paste; brown.
5. Add turmeric, chilli, cumin, coriander powders + salt.
6. Add tomato-cashew puree; cook till oil separates; add curd.
7. Add fried cauliflower + potato, 1 cup water; cook 10 min.
8. Finish garam masala, coriander, kasuri methi.
