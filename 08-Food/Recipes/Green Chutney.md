## Ingredients
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Ginger]] | small piece
- [[Cilantro]] | ½ cup (with mint)
- [[Mint leaves]] | ½ cup (with coriander)
- [[Lemon]] | juice of 1

## Directions
1. Add all ingredients, with salt and 1 tbsp water, to a blender.
2. Blend to a fresh, vibrant green chutney of your desired consistency.
