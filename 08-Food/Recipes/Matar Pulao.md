## Ingredients
- [[Basmati rice]] | 1 cup
- [[Ghee]] | 1½ tbsp
- [[Onion]] | 2, thinly sliced
- [[Green chilli]] | 2 slit + 2 for paste
- [[Ginger]] | 1 piece (for paste)
- [[Garlic]] | 4 cloves (for paste)
- [[Frozen peas]] | 200g
- [[Bay leaf]] | 1
- [[Cinnamon]] | 1 piece
- [[Cloves]] | few
- [[Elaichi full]] | 2-3
- [[Cumin seeds]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Coconut milk]] | 1¼ cups, diluted
- [[Mint leaves]] | optional

## Directions
1. Wash the basmati rice and soak 30 min. Coarsely grind 2 green chillies, ginger and garlic.
2. In a pressure cooker heat 1½ tbsp ghee + 2 tsp oil; add bay leaf, cinnamon, clove, cardamom and cumin seeds.
3. Add the sliced onions; sauté till transparent, then add the slit green chillies and the ginger-garlic-chilli paste.
4. Add peas and 1 tsp salt; cook ~5 min.
5. Add the soaked rice and mix gently. Pour in the diluted coconut milk (or water) and add mint if using.
6. Pressure-cook: once steam builds, put the weight on and cook 1 whistle on medium.
