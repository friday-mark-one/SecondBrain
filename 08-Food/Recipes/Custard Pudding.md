## Ingredients
- [[Bread]] | 10-12 slices (milk bread/brioche)
- [[Ghee]] | 3 tbsp (or butter, for toasting)
- [[Milk]] | 5 cups whole (4½ hot + ½ cold)
- [[Milk powder]] | ¼ cup
- [[Custard powder]] | 5-6 tbsp (vanilla)
- [[Sugar]] | ½-¾ cup
- [[Elaichi full]] | ½ tsp, ground at home (cardamom powder)
- [[Saffron]] | pinch (optional)
- [[Strawberry]] | sliced, to garnish
- [[Almonds]] | chopped, to garnish
- [[Pista]] | chopped, to garnish

## Directions
1. **Toast the bread:** trim the crusts, lightly spread ghee/butter, toast until golden and firm. Lay flat in the serving dish.
2. **Cold slurry:** whisk 5-6 tbsp custard powder into ½ cup cold milk until smooth and lump-free; set aside.
3. **Simmer milk:** bring 4½ cups milk to a gentle boil, lower to a simmer, and stir in the sugar, cardamom and saffron until the sugar dissolves.
4. **Milk powder:** whisk ¼ cup milk powder into a splash of the warm milk to a smooth paste, then stir it back into the pot.
5. **Thicken:** stir the cold slurry, then slowly pour it into the hot milk while whisking on low 2-3 min until it coats the back of a spoon. Turn off the heat.
6. **Assemble & chill:** pour the hot custard over the toasted bread until submerged; cool 15 min, press plastic wrap onto the surface, and refrigerate 6-8 h / overnight.
7. Garnish with strawberries, almonds and pistachios only at serving.
