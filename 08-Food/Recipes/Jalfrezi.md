## Ingredients
- [[Salted butter]] | 1 tbsp
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Onion]] | 1 (½ petals, ½ chopped)
- [[Beans]] | 10
- [[Carrot]] | ½, striped
- [[Tomato]] | 2-2.5 total
- [[Frozen peas]] | 3 spoons
- [[Baby corn]] | 5, chopped
- [[Capsicum]] | 1.5, strips
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 1 tsp
- [[Kasuri methi]] | 1 tsp
- [[Garlic]] | 3, chopped
- [[Ginger]] | 1 inch, julienne
- [[Green chilli]] | 1, slit
- [[Turmeric]] | ½ tsp
- [[Red chilli powder]] | 1 tsp
- [[Coriander powder]] | 1 tsp
- [[Tomato ketchup]] | 2 spoons
- [[Garam masala]] | ½ tsp
- [[Pepper powder]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Butter; sauté onion petals, beans, carrot, tomato strips, peas, baby corn, capsicum on high 5 min; set aside.
2. Oil tadka: jeera, kasuri methi, garlic, ginger, slit chilli.
3. Add chopped onion, brown.
4. Add turmeric, chilli powder, coriander powder, salt, chopped tomato; cook mushy.
5. Add ketchup, then sautéed veg, garam masala, pepper; toss on high.
6. Garnish coriander.
