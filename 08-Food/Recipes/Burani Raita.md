## Ingredients
- [[Curd]] | ~1 cup, beaten
- [[Garlic]] | 2 tbsp (50-60g), finely chopped
- [[Ghee]] | ½ tsp
- [[Red chilli powder]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Cumin powder]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Cilantro]] | for garnish

## Directions
1. Heat ½ tsp ghee; add 2 tbsp finely chopped garlic.
2. Cook 7-8 min to a golden brown, stirring so it doesn't burn.
3. Add ¼ tsp red chilli powder, ¼ tsp jeera powder and salt; toss the masala into the garlic.
4. Stir into the beaten curd; garnish with coriander.
