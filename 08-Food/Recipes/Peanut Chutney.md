## Ingredients
- [[Blanched peanuts]] | 1.3 cups, roasted
- [[Roasted gram]] | ~⅓ of peanuts
- [[Garlic]] | 6 cloves
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Green chilli]] | 8
- [[Coconut]] | 3 spoons
- [[Tamarind paste]] | 2 spoons
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for roasting & tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka

## Directions
1. Roast peanuts; roast green chillies in oil.
2. Grind peanuts, chillies, tamarind paste, garlic with salt + water.
3. Tadka with mustard in the chilli oil.
