## Ingredients
- [[Toor dhal]] | ½ cup, cooked
- [[Coconut]] | 1 small lid
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 1 tsp
- [[Green chilli]] | 2-3
- [[Peppercorns]] | 2-3 corns
- [[Roasted gram]] | 2-3 tsp
- [[Coconut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Asafotida]] | a pinch
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 1-2
- [[Spinach]] | cut, 1 bunch

## Directions
1. Cook ½ cup toor dal.
2. Grind coconut, jeera, green chilli, pepper, odachakadalai into a paste.
3. Tadka: coconut oil, mustard, asafotida, jeera, dry chilli.
4. Add cut palak, cooked dal, the paste, salt; simmer.
