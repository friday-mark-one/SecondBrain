## Ingredients
- [[Toor dhal]] | 1 cup, soaked
- [[Channa dal]] | ¼ cup, soaked
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 6
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Cilantro]] | few
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Turmeric]] | pinch
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Beans]] | 250g
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Black urad dal]] | for tadka

## Directions
1. Soak toor dal + chana dal + dry chilli 1h in hot water.
2. Coarse-grind with salt, coriander, curry leaves, turmeric, asafotida (minimal water).
3. Steam as vada, then crumble in mixer to make usili.
4. Boil 250g beans with salt.
5. Tadka: oil, mustard, asafotida, chana dal, urad dal, dry chilli; add beans then usili, fry 5 min.
