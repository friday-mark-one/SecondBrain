## Ingredients
- [[Dates]] | 10, pitted & chopped
- [[Tamarind paste]] | ½ cup
- [[Red chilli powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Cumin powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Coriander powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Black salt]] | ½ tsp
- [[Dry ginger powder]] | ½ tsp
- [[Jaggery]] | 2 tsp, powdered

## Directions
1. Combine the chopped pitted dates, tamarind puree and water in a pan.
2. Cook on medium until the dates soften.
3. Stir in the dry spices (red chilli, cumin, coriander, black salt, dry ginger, salt) and the powdered jaggery.
4. Cook on low for another 5 min.
5. Cool slightly, then blend into a smooth paste.
