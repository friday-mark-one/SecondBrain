## Ingredients
- [[Dosa batter]] | as needed
- [[Onion]] | ½, to rub on the griddle
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for the griddle and edges

## Directions
1. Oil the griddle and rub a cut onion over it.
2. Pour and spread the dosa batter.
3. Add oil to the edges.
