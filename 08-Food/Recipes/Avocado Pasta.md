## Ingredients
- [[Avocado]] | 1, scooped
- [[Basil leaves]] | 1 box (~15g)
- [[Lemon]] | ~½ (3-4 spoons juice)
- [[Olive oil]] | 2 spoons
- [[Garlic]] | 2 cloves
- [[Pepper powder]] | to taste
- [[Pasta]] | cooked
- [[Chilli flakes]] | to garnish
- [[Italian seasoning]] | to garnish
- [[Cheese]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Grind avocado, basil, lemon juice, olive oil, garlic, salt, pepper.
2. Cook pasta ~15 min.
3. Toss pasta in the avocado sauce.
4. Garnish chilli flakes, seasoning, cheese.
