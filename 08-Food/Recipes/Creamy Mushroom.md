## Ingredients
- [[Baby bella mushrooms]] | 1 small pack
- [[Garlic]] | ~50% of mushroom
- [[Salted butter]] | 2 tbsp
- [[Italian seasoning]] | to taste
- [[Basil leaves]] | few
- [[Heavy whipping cream]] | as needed
- [[Milk]] | ½ cup
- [[Parmesan cheese]] | grated, to taste
- [[Bread]] | to serve

## Directions
1. Clean, peel and cube mushrooms; chop garlic.
2. Butter + garlic, roast light brown; add mushrooms.
3. Add salt, Italian seasoning, basil; mushrooms release water.
4. After 2-3 min add whipping cream + milk; simmer creamy.
5. Stir in grated parmesan; serve with toasted bread.
