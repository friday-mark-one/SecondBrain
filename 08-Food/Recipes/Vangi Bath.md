## Ingredients
- [[Brinjal]] | striped purple type
- [[Tamarind paste]] | 1 spoon
- [[Coriander seeds]] | 2 tsp
- [[Channa dal]] | 1 tsp
- [[Black urad dal]] | ½ tsp
- [[Cumin seeds]] | ½ tsp
- [[Peppercorns]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Fenugreek seeds]] | 10 pieces
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 5
- [[Coconut]] | 4 tsp
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Turmeric]] | pinch
- [[Jaggery]] | small piece
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish
- [[Crystal sona masoori rice]] | cooked

## Directions
1. Cut brinjal into salt water; keep tamarind paste ready.
2. Roast dhaniya, chana dal, urad dal, jeera, pepper, fenugreek, dry chilli, coconut, asafotida; grind to masala.
3. Tadka: mustard, dals, asafotida, green chilli, turmeric.
4. Add brinjal, sauté on medium; salt; cook 7-10 min.
5. Add tamarind paste, jaggery; cook 5-7 min.
6. Add masala and cooked rice; garnish coriander.
