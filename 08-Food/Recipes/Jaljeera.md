## Ingredients
- [[Lemon]] | ½, squeezed
- [[Club soda]] | to top up
- [[Mint leaves]] | handful, crushed
- [[Sugar]] | to taste
- [[Jaljeera powder]] | 1-2 tsp
- [[Boondi]] | for garnish

## Directions
1. Squeeze lemon, add soda, crushed mint leaves and sugar (if needed).
2. Stir in jaljeera powder to taste.
3. Garnish with boondi.
