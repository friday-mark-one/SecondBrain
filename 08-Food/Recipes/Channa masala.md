## Ingredients
- [[Channa]] | 1 cup, soaked 8h
- [[Onion]] | 1
- [[Tomato]] | 2 cups puree
- [[Green chilli]] | 1, slit
- [[Ghee]] | for cooking
- [[Elaichi full]] | 3 pods
- [[Bay leaf]] | 1
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 1 tsp
- [[Ginger garlic paste]] | 1 tsp
- [[Channa masala]] | 1 tsp
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish
- [[Lemon]] | to serve

## Directions
1. Soak channa 8h; pressure-cook 10 min with salt.
2. Ghee + elaichi, bay leaf, jeera.
3. Add chopped onion, sauté; add slit chilli.
4. Add ginger garlic paste, brown well.
5. Add channa masala + salt; add tomato puree, cook till oil separates.
6. Add cooked channa + water; cook 10 min.
7. Garnish coriander; serve with onion + lemon.
