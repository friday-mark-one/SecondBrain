## Ingredients
- [[Rice sevai]] | soaked
- [[Coconut]] | grated, as needed
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Mustard]] | for tempering
- [[Channa dal]] | for tempering
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tempering

## Directions
1. Soak rice sevai in hot water for 5 min.
2. In a pan, temper mustard seeds, chana dal, asafoetida, curry leaves, and slit green chillies.
3. Add the grated coconut.
4. Add salt to taste.
5. Add the soaked sevai and mix thoroughly.
