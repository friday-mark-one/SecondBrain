## Ingredients
- [[Black urad dal]] | 2 spoons
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 1
- [[Ginger]] | small piece
- [[Tamarind paste]] | small
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Coconut]] | 2 spoons
- [[Mint leaves]] | 1 bunch
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Channa dal]] | for tadka

## Directions
1. Dry-roast urad dal + dry chilli.
2. Add ginger, tamarind paste, green chilli, coconut.
3. Sauté mint leaves separately.
4. Grind with salt (minimal water).
5. Tadka: mustard, asafotida, chana dal.
