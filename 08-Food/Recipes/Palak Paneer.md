## Ingredients
- [[Paneer]] | cut, soaked
- [[Onion]] | 1 medium
- [[Tomato]] | 1
- [[Spinach]] | ½ box baby spinach
- [[Ginger]] | 1 inch
- [[Garlic]] | 4 cloves
- [[Green chilli]] | 5 small
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 1 tsp
- [[Cinnamon]] | 1 piece
- [[Elaichi full]] | 3-4 pods
- [[Bay leaf]] | 1
- [[Kasuri methi]] | 1 tsp + extra
- [[Ghee]] | for cooking
- [[Garam masala]] | ½ tsp
- [[Fresh Cream]] | to finish

## Directions
1. Soak cut paneer in warm salt water.
2. Blanch spinach 2-3 min; cool in cold water.
3. Blend spinach with ginger, garlic, green chilli.
4. Optionally fry paneer; set aside.
5. Ghee + jeera, cinnamon, elaichi, bay leaf, kasuri methi.
6. Sauté onion, then tomato till mushy.
7. Add palak paste, salt, water; add paneer; simmer 5 min.
8. Finish with garam masala, kasuri methi, fresh cream.
