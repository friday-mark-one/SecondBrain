## Ingredients
- [[Moong dal]] | 1 cup
- [[Turmeric]] | pinch
- [[Green chilli]] | 3, split
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Toor dhal]] | for tadka
- [[Dry red chilli]] | optional
- [[Lemon]] | to finish

## Directions
1. Cook 1 cup moong dal with turmeric (Instapot 5 min).
2. Tadka: oil, toor dal, 3 split chillies.
3. Squeeze lemon to finish.
