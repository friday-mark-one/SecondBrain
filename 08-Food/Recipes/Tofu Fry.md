## Ingredients
- [[Tofu]] | 1 pack (450g)
- [[Corn flour]] | 3-4 spoons

## Directions
1. Cube 1 packet of tofu (450g ≈ 5 servings of 90g).
2. Toss with corn flour and ¾ spoon salt to coat.
3. Air-fry at 400°F for 15 min, shaking at the halfway mark.
