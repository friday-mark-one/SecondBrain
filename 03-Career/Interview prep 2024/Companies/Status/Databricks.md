- E-Ink Waveform Architecture Redesign: I led a cross-functional team of 3 engineers (including a hardware and a kernel engineer) to redesign Eink waveform architecture (C/C++). This project spanned 3 months and reduced ghosting of text & images and improved page turn performance by 12%. I defined the overall strategy, owned the implementation within the rendering engine and drove the project to completion with stakeholder approvals.
