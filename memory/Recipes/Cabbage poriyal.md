Ingredients
Groundnut oil | 2 tsp
Mustard seeds | ½ tsp
Chana dal | 1 tsp
Asafoetida | a pinch
Green chili or dry red chili | 1-2 (as per requirement)
Vegetable of choice | chopped, as required
Salt | to taste
Curry powder | as required
Directions
Tadka: Heat groundnut oil in a pan, add mustard seeds, chana dal, asafoetida, and chili; let it splutter.
Add the cut vegetables of choice and sauté.
Add salt and cook until the vegetables are done.
Add any required curry powder, mix well, and serve.
