## Ingredients
- [[Coriander seeds]] | 2 tsp, soaked
- [[Toor dhal]] | 2 tsp, soaked
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 2 tsp
- [[Green chilli]] | 6
- [[Ginger]] | small piece
- [[Coconut]] | 4 tsp
- [[Coconut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Turmeric]] | ½ tsp
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Cilantro]] | few
- [[Bhindi]] | cut, 1 cup
- [[Curd]] | 100 ml

## Directions
1. Soak dhaniya + toor dal 2h; grind with jeera, green chilli, ginger, coconut into paste.
2. Coconut oil tadka: mustard, jeera, turmeric, asafotida, curry + coriander leaves.
3. Add cut okra, pinch salt, sauté till roasted.
4. Add 100 ml curd, cook 1 min.
5. Add masala paste + salt; boil once only.
