## Ingredients
- [[Black urad dal]] | 1 cup, soaked 8h
- [[Rajma]] | ¼ cup, soaked 8h
- [[Turmeric]] | ¼ tsp
- [[Red chilli powder]] | 1 spoon
- [[Salted butter]] | for cooking
- [[Tomato]] | 2, puree
- [[Ginger garlic paste]] | 2 spoons
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tempering
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Coriander powder]] | 1 spoon
- [[Cumin powder]] | ½ spoon
- [[Garam masala]] | ½ spoon
- [[Kasuri methi]] | to finish
- [[Fresh Cream]] | to finish
- [[Ghee]] | for tempering

## Directions
1. Soak urad dal + rajma 8h; pressure-cook 10-12 min with salt, turmeric, chilli, butter; mash lightly.
2. Ghee + butter, jeera, asafotida.
3. Add ginger paste, sauté; add turmeric, chilli powder.
4. Add 2 cups tomato puree; cook till oil separates.
5. Add coriander + jeera powder.
6. Add cooked dal, water, salt; cook 10 min.
7. Finish garam masala, kasuri methi, fresh cream, butter.
