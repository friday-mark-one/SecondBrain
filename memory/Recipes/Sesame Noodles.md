## Ingredients
- [[Lo mein noodles]] | 2-3 sets
- [[Tahini]] | 6 tbsp
- [[Sesame oil]] | 1.5 tbsp
- [[Soy sauce]] | 4 tbsp
- [[Sugar]] | 1.5 spoons
- [[Black vinegar]] | 4 tbsp
- [[Spring onion]] | to garnish
- [[Sesame seeds]] | toasted, to garnish
- [[Chilli oil]] | to garnish
- [[Blanched peanuts]] | crushed, to garnish

## Directions
1. Boil noodles 3 min; drain, reserve the water.
2. Sauce: whisk tahini, sesame oil, soy sauce, sugar, black vinegar.
3. Whisk in ~16 tbsp noodle water to adjust consistency.
4. Toss hot noodles in sauce.
5. Garnish scallions, toasted sesame, chilli oil, crushed peanuts.
