## Ingredients
- [[Potato]] | 3, boiled
- [[Carrot]] | 2, boiled
- [[Cauliflower]] | ½, boiled
- [[Beans]] | 10, boiled
- [[Frozen peas]] | ½ cup
- [[Onion]] | 2 (1 mashed-veg, 1 for cooking)
- [[Tomato]] | 2
- [[Salted butter]] | 50g + extra
- [[Red chilli powder]] | 1 tsp
- [[Turmeric]] | ½ tsp
- [[Coriander powder]] | 1 tsp
- [[Ginger garlic paste]] | 1.5 tsp
- [[Capsicum]] | 1
- [[Pav bhaji masala]] | to taste
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish
- [[Lemon]] | to serve
- [[Pav]] | to serve

## Directions
1. Boil and mash potatoes, carrots, cauliflower, beans, peas.
2. Melt butter; add chilli powder, turmeric, coriander powder.
3. Add finely chopped onion, sauté with a little water.
4. Add ginger garlic paste, cook off raw smell.
5. Add chopped tomatoes + water, cook till mushy.
6. Add chopped capsicum, cook 1 min.
7. Add mashed veg, pav bhaji masala, salt; simmer.
8. Garnish with coriander; serve with pav, lemon, chopped onion, butter.
