## Ingredients
- [[Rava]] | ⅔ cup (double-roasted, for 2)
- [[Onion]] | 1
- [[Carrot]] | as needed
- [[Beans]] | as needed
- [[Potato]] | as needed
- [[Tomato]] | as needed
- [[Capsicum]] | as needed
- [[Mustard]] | for tempering
- [[Channa dal]] | for tempering
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 2
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tempering

## Directions
1. Temper mustard seeds, chana dal, and dry red chillies with asafoetida.
2. Add the onion, carrot, beans, potato, tomato, and capsicum; sauté.
3. Add water (1 : 2.5–3 by volume) and salt.
4. Add the rava and cook, stirring to avoid lumps.
