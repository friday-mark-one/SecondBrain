## Ingredients
- [[Groundnut oil]] | 2 tbsp
- [[Mustard]] | ½ tsp
- [[Asafotida]] | a pinch
- [[Fenugreek seeds]] | pinch (fenugreek)
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Bhindi]] | or veg of choice
- [[Brinjal]] | optional
- [[Carrot]] | optional
- [[Pearl onions]] | optional
- [[Drumstick]] | optional
- [[Toor dhal]] | ¾ cup, cooked
- [[Tamarind paste]] | ~150 ml tamarind water
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Tadka: oil, mustard, asafotida, fenugreek powder, curry leaves.
2. Add vegetables of choice and sauté; salt + water per veg (none for okra/garlic; little for brinjal/onion/capsicum).
3. Add cooked dal (¾ cup).
4. Add tamarind water (~150 ml) and salt (~1.25 tsp).
5. Boil; garnish with coriander.
