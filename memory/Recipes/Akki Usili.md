## Ingredients
- [[Crystal sona masoori rice]] | 1 cup
- [[Moong dal]] | ½ cup
- [[Coconut]] | ¼ cup
- [[Coconut oil]] | for tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tadka
- [[Urad dal]] | for tadka
- [[Channa dal]] | for tadka
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Curry leaves]] | few
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 1
- [[Green chilli]] | 1

## Directions
1. Dry-roast rice and moong dal separately on tawa.
2. Add salt and coconut.
3. Tadka: coconut oil, mustard, jeera, urad dal, chana dal, asafotida, curry leaves, dry + green chilli.
4. Cook everything in Instapot (usual water + time).
