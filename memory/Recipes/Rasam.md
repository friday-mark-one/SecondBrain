## Ingredients
- [[Tomato]] | 1, boiled & smashed
- [[Toor dhal]] | ~100 ml dal water
- [[Tamarind paste]] | ~75 ml tamarind water
- [[Sambar powder]] | 2 tsp
- [[Asafotida]] | a pinch
- [[Pepper jeera powder]] | to taste
- [[Ghee]] | for tadka
- [[Mustard]] | for tadka
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tadka
- [[Cilantro]] | to garnish

## Directions
1. Smash boiled tomato into the pan.
2. Add dal water, tamarind water, salt, sambar powder, asafotida.
3. Cover and let it boil; remove from flame.
4. Add pepper-jeera powder.
5. Tadka with ghee, mustard, jeera; add coriander leaves.
