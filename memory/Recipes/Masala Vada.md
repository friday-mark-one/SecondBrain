## Ingredients
- [[Channa dal]] | 150-200g, soaked 1.5-2h
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 6 round
- [[Garlic]] | 10 cloves
- [[Curry leaves]] | 10
- [[Fennel seeds]] | ½ tsp
- [[Onion]] | 1, finely chopped
- [[Cilantro]] | finely chopped
- [[Asafotida]] | ½ tsp
- [[Green chilli]] | to taste
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for deep frying

## Directions
1. Soak chana dal, dried red chilli, garlic, curry leaves and fennel seeds for 1.5-2 h.
2. Drain the water completely; coarse-grind WITHOUT adding any water.
3. Mix in finely chopped onion, coriander, asafotida, green chilli and salt.
4. Shape into vadas and deep-fry in oil till golden.
