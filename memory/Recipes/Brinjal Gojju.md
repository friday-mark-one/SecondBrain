## Ingredients
- [[Coriander seeds]] | 4 spoons
- [[Channa dal]] | 2 spoons
- [[Urad dal]] | 2 spoons
- [[Peppercorns]] | 1 spoon
- [[Cumin seeds]] | 1 spoon
- [[Dry red chilli]] | 5
- [[Brinjal]] | 3, cut
- [[Tomato]] | 1
- [[Asafotida]] | pinch
- [[Turmeric]] | pinch
- [[Tamarind paste]] | 1 spoon
- [[Groundnut oil]] | for tadka

## Directions
1. Dry-roast coriander, chana dal, urad dal, pepper, jeera, dry chilli; powder it.
2. Tadka with dals, asafotida, turmeric.
3. Add brinjal, sauté.
4. Add tomato, smash.
5. Add tamarind paste, water, salt and the curry powder.
