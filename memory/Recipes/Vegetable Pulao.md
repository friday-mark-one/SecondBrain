## Ingredients
- [[Cilantro]] | ½ cup
- [[Mint leaves]] | ¼ cup
- [[Ginger]] | 1 inch
- [[Garlic]] | 2 cloves
- [[Green chilli]] | 2
- [[Elaichi full]] | 2
- [[Cinnamon]] | 1 inch
- [[Cloves]] | 3-5
- [[Fennel seeds]] | 1 spoon
- [[Onion]] | ½, sliced
- [[Tomato]] | ½, diced
- [[Beans]] | few
- [[Potato]] | ½
- [[Frozen peas]] | ¼ cup
- [[Carrot]] | ½
- [[Capsicum]] | ½
- [[Ghee]] | 2 spoons
- [[Cumin seeds]] | for tempering
- [[Bay leaf]] | 1
- [[Cashews]] | few
- [[Peppercorns]] | ½ spoon
- [[Basmati rice]] | cooked

## Directions
1. Grind coriander, mint, ginger, garlic, green chilli, cardamom, cinnamon, fennel into paste.
2. Steam beans, potato, peas, carrot, capsicum.
3. Ghee + jeera, cinnamon, bay leaf, cardamom, cloves, pepper, cashews on low.
4. Sauté sliced onion till light brown; add diced tomato.
5. Add cooked veg, then the paste; cook 2 min.
6. Mix in cooked rice.
