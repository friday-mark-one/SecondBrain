## Location-based Peer Assist

- For example, you want to buy pen from a stationary store and you are currently busy; you save this in the app.  If any of your friends come in the vicinity of the store (detected using GPS), they get a notification in their app. They can buy it for you and give it to you. (You can control which of your friends get this notification.)
- Same thing can be done for food from a specific restaurant, theater tickets, books from a particular shop, ladoo from tirupati, chocolates from Switzerland.
- Also you can use this app as a reminder for yourself whenever you come to a location you wanted to go and get something.
